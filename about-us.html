About Us

Welcome to Help You Fit, a fitness company dedicated to helping beginners start and succeed in their fitness journey. We believe that everyone has the potential to achieve their health and fitness goals, regardless of their current fitness level.

Our team consists of six Information Technology students studying at Southern Alberta Institute of Technology. Although we are in the IT industry, our passion for fitness should not be underestimated.

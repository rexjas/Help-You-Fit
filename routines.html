Bro Split


The bro split routine involves working on one or two muscle groups every day, typically with high volume and low frequency. It's a well-known exercise plan associated with bodybuilding culture and is effective for gaining muscle mass. However, it may not be the best option for improving general strength and functional fitness.

Upper-Lower-Split


The upper and lower body split program separates the body into two sections, alternately working the muscles in each section on different days. This strategy makes it possible to train in a balanced and effective manner, avoiding overtraining and exhaustion while still encouraging muscle growth and strength increases. It is appropriate for both novice and seasoned lifters who want to increase their general fitness and develop a well-balanced body.
